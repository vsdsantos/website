Biography

Hello! Welcome to my page. I’m a passionate student in the aerospace engineering field and currently works as a stress analysis intern at Embraer. My research interests includes aerospace structures, composite and laminated structures, aeroelasticity and novel computational methods.

I’ve worked, so far, with software development (academic and professionally) and with project management. Mostly technologies that I’m used to work are Python, Julia, C#, JS and VBA. Furthermore, I’ve experience in FEM modelling of metallic and composite structures in Nastran with Femap and Altair software suits.
